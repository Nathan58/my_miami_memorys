My Miami Memorys

A Visitors Guide

Art Deco Splender

The splendor of art deco architecture, in hotels takes you back, to the 40s and 50s era, where Miami was the Hub of the Southern Elegance, before you headed to your other Caribbean destinations. Cuba, Puerto Rico, and all Islands south of South Beach. Come take in everything there is, in southern architectural culture.

Miami Beach Style

As America's one-and-only 20th century Metropolis, Miami is largely defined by its modern architecture and art deco design. South Beach quickly filled up with architecture designs known today as tropical art deco. Begin your tour of Miami at the Art Deco capital of the world, Coral Gables.

Miami's Timeless History

Since 1912 when the Lummus Brothers, Miami businessman establish an Ocean Beach Realty Company, and envisioned a city set against the backdrop of the ocean made up of modest single families, Miami Beach has grown from that dream into a major tourist attraction. In the 1920s Miami was known as a Tropical Paradise for tourist. In the 1950s the population hit 172,000. It's been growing ever since. Miami, Paradise destination of the South.
