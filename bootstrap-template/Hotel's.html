Art Deco Splender

The splendor of art deco architecture, in hotels takes you back, to the 40s and 50s era, where Miami was the Hub of the Southern Elegance, before you headed to your other Caribbean destinations. Cuba, Puerto Rico, and all Islands south of South Beach. Come take in everything there is, in southern architectural culture.

Miami Beach Style

With a laid back, relaxed kind of vibe, you can never be too at ease in this town. Loungeing on a beach, or dinner at a South Beach Bistro, this is 'Miami Beach' style!

Miami's Timeless History

Soon after World War II, many members of the armed services return as permanent citizens tripling the population. Many service members who served in the south pacific, felt right at home; They never left.

When you take your vacation, to make your Miami Memorys, you may not want to leave!
